How to get a free vps as a University Student?

Danushka Herath
1 year ago
Reading Time: 2 minutes

There are some ways to get a free vps for free as university students. I will guide you some ways.

Github

github offers you some valuable student pack for students for free. If you have a university offered email it will be very easy to verify you as a student. If you don’t have a school issued email you can use your university issued ID card for verify you as a student.

go to this link and register : https://education.github.com

within 4 business days you will receive your verification email with all of your pack for free. If you have an idea for a take a vps you have two options in that package.

  1. Digital Ocean
  2. AWS

If you get digital ocean you will have to  provide your credit card info for verification in Digital Ocean. After verification process you’ll have a promo code with 50$ for free one year.

The Lowest Price of Digital Ocean VPS is 5$. You can run VPS for 10 months for free. Performance will be 1GB RAM, 20GB HDD and 1TB bandwidth with linux Operating System.

With registering Amazon Cloud You will have 110$ free for use AWS cloud. For vps instance you can create a EC2 instance. Linux/Windows and many operating systems to install as you wish.

Google cloud platform will give you 300$ free trial. This Service Not require studentship of a university. Any person who can register at https://cloud.google.com will have 300$ trial for 1 Year. Compute engine for VPS instance. For 1 instance there are some plans for 4$/mo and 30$/mo to select as you want. I personally recommend this cloud service.

If you know any other service like this for students feel free to comment below.
